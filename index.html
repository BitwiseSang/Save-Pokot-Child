WHO ARE WE

Our Story

We are a passionate, grassroots non-profit organization committed to transforming the lives of marginalized children in Pokot, Kenya. Our journey began with a simple but powerful belief — that every child, regardless of where they’re born, deserves access to education, safety, nourishment, and the chance to dream freely. Our team is made up of local community leaders, educators, healthcare workers, and volunteers from all walks of life who are unified by a shared vision: to create lasting change at the grassroots level by empowering children and their families. Through education programs, feeding initiatives, clean water projects, and mentorship, we work hand in hand with the community to uplift and inspire. When you support our work, you’re not just donating to a cause — you’re becoming part of a movement.A movement that believes the forgotten corners of the world deserve the brightest hope.

MEET OUR TEAM

Sarah Naliaka

Founder & Executive director

Sarah grew up in the Pokot region and founded the organization after witnessing first-hand the effects of poverty and lack of access to education. With a background in humanitarian work and community organizing, she leads the NGO with vision, compassion, and bold action.

David Kiprop

Community Outreach Coordinator

David is the heart of our local engagement. He works directly with families, schools, and village elders to ensure every child has access to the support they need. His ability to build trust has opened doors across remote areas of Pokot.

Mercy Chebet

Founder & Executive director

Mercy designs and manages our education centers and literacy initiatives. As a former teacher, she’s passionate about turning under-resourced spaces into thriving learning hubs for children who have never stepped foot in a classroom.
